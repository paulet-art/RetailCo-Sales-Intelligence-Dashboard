Key Insights (from the data)
Electronics dominates revenue at $2.43M (59.5%) but has the lowest margin at 39.9%
Sports and Food & Beverage carry the highest margins at 61% and 59.8% respectively
2023 saw a -16.6% revenue dip, recovering +6.4% in 2024
December is consistently the strongest month across all 3 years
Chicago and Tokyo are the top-performing stores by revenue
In-Store slightly outperforms Online in revenue (52.3% share) but both channels track closely
